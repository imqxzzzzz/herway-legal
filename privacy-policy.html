Her Way — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Her Way ("the app") helps you track your cycle, pregnancy, postpartum recovery, nutrition, and fitness. Your privacy is the foundation of the app.

What we collect

  • Account info: your email address and name, to create and secure your account.
  • Health and wellness data you choose to log: cycle dates, symptoms, pregnancy and postpartum entries, meals, workouts, hydration, journals, and notes.
  • Apple Health data (steps, exercise minutes) only if you grant permission — used solely to show your activity in the app.
  • Messages you send to the AI companion, used to generate a reply.
  • Anonymous usage and crash data that helps us fix bugs and improve the app.

How we use it

Your data is used only to run the app's features for you. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with data brokers. We do not use your health data for advertising. The app does not track you across other apps or websites.

Where it lives

Your data is stored securely with Google Firebase (our database provider) tied to your private account. Belly photos are stored only on your device. Weather uses your approximate location for the moment of the request and is never stored.

Your control

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time in the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account. You can revoke Apple Health or location access anytime in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions

Her Way Premium is billed through Apple. We never see or store your payment details.
